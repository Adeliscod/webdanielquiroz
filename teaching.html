On July 2016, I obtained a Postgraduate Certificate in Higher Education from the LSE, becoming an Associate Fellow of the Higher Education Academy.

From January 2014 to June 2017, I taught MA107 (on Michaelmas terms) and MA207 (on Lent terms), at the London School of Economics. These courses provide basic calculus and linear algebra tools, with an emphasis on applications to economics.

Also at LSE, I was part of the Maths Support Centre staff, from October 2015 to June 2017.

During the academic year 2009/10, I was an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant at Universidad Simón Bolívar, Caracas, Venezuela. That year, I taught a Linear Algebra course for first year Engineering and Mathematics students, and a Probability course for second and third year Engineering students.
